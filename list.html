Product Status Definitions

  • In Stock - Quantity is above reorder level
  • Low Stock - Quantity is below reorder level
  • Out of Stock - Quantity is zero

Stock Management Tips

  • Review low-stock items regularly
  • Conduct periodic inventory audits
  • Update product information as prices change
  • Track supplier performance and lead times
  • Use FIFO method for perishable items
